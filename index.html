Hello & Welcome

The Paw Pad is a one on one dog grooming service based in lytham. Your dog will be treated like one of my own. Whilst your dog is having their bespoke treatment, we will more than likely be listening to radio 2. It'll often help spark a lively debate between myself and your four legged friend on a wide range of hot topics as well as pop master!.

After their desired treatment is complete they will receive a bucket load of praise and then it's off to the enclosed garden for a small adventure and well deserved toilet brake. If its a day of rain then we will wait inside and read or play and wait for your return.

For now please take a look around the site, view the gallery and read the comments. If you have any questions or concerns please get in touch as we will always try to be accommodating.
