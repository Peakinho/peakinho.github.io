Puppy Introduction

£20-25

The puppy introduction allows your dog at a young age to familiarise your dog with the smells and sounds of a groomers. It also allows for a relationship of trust to be built between the groomer and dog. Making future visits much easier and more enjoyable for everyone all round.

The Puppy package includes

  • A trip in the bath, where there feet will be wet as they get used to the experience
  • A dry with a towel and then the hair dryer on his/her paws
  • A good brush and check for any early signs of matting
  • eyes cleaned any debris removed and excess hair cut away
  • Nails and ears checked
  • Finish with fragrance and lots of praise
